Step {{step}}: Copy Descriptor to CDs

  1. Insert one of your blank "Descriptor" CDs.
  2. Open the Files application and click on Documents.
  3. Copy the file named "descriptor.txt" to the CD and click Write to Disc and then click Burn.
  4. When you see "Data CD successfully burnt" click Close and remove the CD.
  5. Repeat steps 1 through 4 above with your other six "Descriptor" CDs then click Next to continue.
  6. {{instructions}}

Ensure that you have seven identical descriptor.txt files on all seven CDs labeled "Descriptor" before moving to your Primary Laptop.
