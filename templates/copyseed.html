Step {{step}}: Copy Seeds to CDs

  1. Insert one of your five blank CDs.
  2. Open the Files application and click on Documents.
  3. Copy the folder named "yhseed.txt" to the CD and click Write to Disc and then click Burn Several Copies.
  4. When you see "Data CD successfully burnt" click Close and remove and then label the CD "Level 1 Seed"
  5. Repeat steps 1 through 4 so that you have a total of 5 CDs containing the file "yhseed.txt" and then click Next to continue.

Ensure that you have a "yhseed.text" on each of your five CDs labeled "Seed" before clicking Next.
