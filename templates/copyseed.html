Step {{step}}: Copy Seed to CDs

  1. Insert one of your five blank "Seed" CDs.
  2. Open the Files application and click on Documents.
  3. Copy the file named "yetiseed.txt" to the CD and click Write to Disc and then click Burn.
  4. When you see "Data CD successfully burnt" click Close and remove the CD.
  5. Repeat steps 1 through 4 so that you have a total of 5 "Seed" CDs containing the same "yetiseed.txt" file and then click Next to continue.

Ensure that you have a "yetiseed.txt" on each of your five CDs labeled "Seed" before clicking Next.
