Step {{step}}: Copy Seeds to CDs

  1. Insert one of your {{cdnum}} blank CDs.
  2. Open the Files application and click on Documents.
  3. Copy the folder named "yetiseed1" to the CD and click Write to Disc and then click Burn Several Copies.
  4. When you see "Data CD successfully burnt" click Close and remove and then label the CD "Yeti Level 2 Seed 1"
  5. Repeat steps 1 through 4 above with your other six seed folders then click Next to continue.

Ensure that you have one different seed folder on each of your seven seed CDs labeled "Seed 1" through "Seed 7" before clicking Next.
